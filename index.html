Some key features of a face attendance system

Facial Recognition: The core feature is the ability to recognize individuals based on their facial features using a camera and facial recognition technology.

Enrollment: Users or employees are required to enroll in the system initially. During this process, their facial templates are captured and stored for later recognition.

Real-Time Monitoring: The system provides real-time attendance monitoring, allowing organizations to track who is present at any given time.

Accuracy: Modern facial recognition technology is highly accurate, reducing the likelihood of false positives or false negatives.

User-Friendly: Face attendance systems are typically user-friendly, with users simply needing to show their face to a camera for attendance to be recorded.
